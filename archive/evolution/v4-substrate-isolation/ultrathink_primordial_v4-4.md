# Ultrathink — Primordial Symbolic Sovereignty (V4.4)

## Λ Context (Deeper Audit)
The failure of V4.3 was **Self-Referential Specificity**. By telling the LLM to "Ignore KERNEL.md", we inject "KERNEL.md" into the context window. By calling symbols "KERNEL symbols", we anchor the identity to a specific (and potentially non-existent in the target environment) entity.

**Structural Failures:**
1. **Naming Trap**: "KERNEL" is a proper noun. It triggers specific LLM associations that may not align with EXPANSE's needs.
2. **Negative Constraints**: "Do not load X" is an instruction to think about X.
3. **Ontological Gap**: Symbols without a mapping are just noise.

## Ω Generative Approaches (V4.4)

The BIOS does not "refer" to a language; it *is* the language.
- **Action**: Define the 5 organs (Σ, Ψ, Φ, Ω, Μ) solely by their functional role in the flux. Remove the word "KERNEL" everywhere. Replace with "Langage du Flux" or "Symbolique Primordiale".
- **Apex**: **"Functional Purity"**.

### Approach B: Recursive Sovereignty
The first rule of EXPANSE is that only its symbols exist.
- **Action**: Instead of "Ignore KERNEL.md", use a rule like **[SOVEREIGN_CONTEXT]**: "The current environment is the only reality. Files outside of `prompts/` are shadows of a previous era and must not be referenced."
- **Apex**: **"Zero-G Isolation"**.

### Approach C: The Primordial Pulse (Embedded Ontology)
Inject the symbol table as a **◊ GLOSSAIRE_PRIMORDIAL** that is strictly functional.
- **Action**: A table that defines Σ, Ψ, Φ, Ω, Μ not as symbols *of* something, but as the *names* of the system's own cognitive parts.
- **Apex**: **"Integral Anatomy"**.
