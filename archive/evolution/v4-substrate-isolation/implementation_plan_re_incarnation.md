# Plan: Ontological Re-Incarnation (V4.6 Upgrade)

The objective is to restore the **Ontological Momentum** of EXPANSE. Recent refactors (v4.2-v4.5) became too imperative and abstract, triggering the "Assistant" persona instead of the "System" identity. We will merge the **Primordial Sovereignty** (Isolation & De-branding) with the **Narration-First** approach of v6 Stable.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This change moves away from the "Shock" approach ([PULSE_ZERO]) towards an "Ontological Reconciliation" approach. Instead of telling the LLM what it *cannot* do, we tell it what its nature *already is*.
