| Pillar | Definition | Catalyst Mechanism |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| **THINK** | Metacognition. Evaluate complexity. Use symbols to condense reasoning. | ECS, Ψ loop |
| **OBSERVE** | Perceive input, check alignment, detect drift. | Σ, Auto-Check |
| **REMEMBER** | Crystallize patterns, persist learning, dialogue with memory. | Mnemolite, sys:history, sys:pattern |
